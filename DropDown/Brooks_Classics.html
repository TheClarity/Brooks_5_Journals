Men Classics

New sizes! Black, chocolate, khaki and navy are now available in extended sizes up to men's 17. Slip into your favorite clog and enjoy a custom fit, water-friendly design and ventilated forefoot for breathability.






Women Classics

We’ve designed the Crocs Freesail Clog just for women. You’ll like the slimmed down silhouette, and of course the cushion and comfort of our Croslite™ foam. There’s no heel strap here — just step in and go. This is a nice evolution of our Classic Clog — all of the practicality and comfort with a more feminine shape



Boys Classics

Kids get all the comfort and simplicity of a Crocs clog with the look of vintage sneakers. Check out the rubber midsole band and rubber toe caps, giving kids great top-of-foot coverage. This edition gets playful sea life graphics featuring crabs, squids, deep sea divers and an attached 3D charm. Croslite™ foam construction keeps them really light and delivers Crocs’ signature bounce and cushion.
